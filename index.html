about Open Banking

The introduction of open APIs to enable third party payment authorisation and to share a bank customer’s account details and transaction history offer an innovation opportunity for banks that want to become more customer-centric. Open APIs offer banks an opportunity to build an ecosystem of partnerships that will better serve them to deliver on their core mission: protecting nances and helping customers build wealth.

The Second Payment Services Directive (PSD2) offers banks a way to avoid disruption and take up the technology opportunities of today. Today, all industries are becoming technology industries, where a bulk of the services and products they offer must be supplied or managed digitally. This new industrial model is creating a platform economy, where everyone is empowered to create their own digital value chains unique to their needs and experiences.

Open Banking will be in the center of the Industrial Revolution 4.0.

selected features

We are hard at work and things will constantly change. However, the global banking industry do share the same needs;

  • /accounts
    List of accounts at that the user has access to.
  • /payments
    Handle payments between accounts.
  • /connect
    Enable clients to grant their own access.
  • /customers
    Get the KYC statuses for a customer over time.
  • /cards
    Data about all the physical cards a user has been issued.
  • /paystats
    Anonymized aggregated card purchase data.
